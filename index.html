Our Story

Blossom Haven began in 2025 as a small flower stall in the local market. Founded by two friends, passionate florists with vision to bring artisanal floral designs to everyday life, our shop has grown into a beloved destination for flower enthusiasts.

We specialize in seasonal, sustainably-sourced flowers arranged by our team of expert florists. Each bouquet is handcrafted with care and attention to detail, ensuring every arrangement tells its own beautiful story.

Visit our website and explore our online collections - where every petal is placed with purpose and every stem tells a story.

Shipping Information

Delivery Information

We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2pm within city limits. Our delivery fee is PKR 300 for standard delivery (3-5 business days) and PKR 600 for express delivery (1-2 business days).

All deliveries include careful handling and a handwritten note. Recipient will receive an SMS notification upon delivery.

Returns Policy

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we cannot accept returns of fresh flowers. However, if you're unsatisfied with your arrangement, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a replacement or store credit.

For non-perishable items (vases, gifts), returns are accepted within 7 days with original packaging.

Privacy Policy

We collect personal information only to process your orders and improve your shopping experience. Your data is never shared with third parties without consent.

We use secure payment processing and encrypt all sensitive information. You may request account deletion or data access at any time by contacting us.
